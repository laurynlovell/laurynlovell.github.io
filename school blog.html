How I'm Preparing for Next School Year This Summer

This summer, I am enjoying time off but also looking forward to teaching a new subject next year and moving classrooms. I will be teaching 5th grade reading this year, so I am having fun finding resources for that. I am also brainstorming ideas for classroom decor.

In July, I will be attending a science professional development conference. All of us 5th grade teachers teach our own homeroom's science, so we will all be participating.

Lauryn Lovell, 2023
